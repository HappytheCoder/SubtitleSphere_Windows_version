• OpenAI Whisper – for local audio transcription using speech recognition models.


• gTTS (Google Text-to-Speech) – for interfacing with Google’s TTS service.

• Deep-Translate (Google Translate via Deep Translate) – for accessing Google Translate APIs.

fairy-root/ComfyUI-OpenAI-FM to provide AI narration with expressive voices and emo-tions.
